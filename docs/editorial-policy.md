# Editorial Policy

## Content Standards

### What We Cover
- AI/ML research announcements
- Product launches from major AI companies
- Open source AI projects
- Industry partnerships and acquisitions
- Technical tutorials and insights

### What We Don't Cover
- Clickbait or sensationalized content
- Unverified claims or rumors
- Investment advice or market predictions
- Personal opinions presented as facts
- Content requiring login to access

## Writing Style

### Tone
- Objective and factual
- Concise and informative
- Professional but accessible

### Language
- Primary language: Chinese
- Include original English titles when relevant
- Use transliteration for proper nouns when helpful

## Quality Guidelines

1. All summaries must be factually accurate
2. No promotional or marketing language
3. Credit original sources clearly
4. Mark opinion pieces clearly
5. Include diverse perspectives when available
